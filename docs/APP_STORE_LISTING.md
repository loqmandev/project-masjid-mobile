#### Check-In Flow

1. Sign in with your own account
2. Find nearby masjid on Home screen
3. Tap masjid → View details
4. Go to "Check In" tab
5. When within 100m, tap "Check In"
6. Wait 5 minutes (timer displays)
7. Tap "Check Out" → Earn points
